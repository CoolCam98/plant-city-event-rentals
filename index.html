Frequently Asked Questions

What do you include for setup?

We arrive about 1 hour before your rental for setup and return after to break down. This setup/teardown time isn’t counted against your package hours.

Do you travel outside Plant City?

Yes—Tampa, Lakeland, Brandon, Valrico, and more. A small travel fee may apply based on distance/parking.

Are you insured?

Yes. We’re insured and can provide a Certificate of Insurance (COI) to your venue upon request.

Are prints and attendants included?

Our base package is digital-first. Attendant and prints are available as add-ons or included in higher-tier packages.

Can we bring our own props?

Yes! You're welcome to bring your own props or decor to match your theme.

What does the venue need to provide?

A 10' x 10' space, a standard power outlet, and clear load-in access. We’ll coordinate timing with your planner or venue contact.
